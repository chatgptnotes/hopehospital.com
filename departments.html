Frequently Asked Questions

Which departments are available at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Hope Hospital provides multiple super specialty departments including Orthopedics, Cardiology, Neurosurgery, Oncology, Nephrology, Gastroenterology, Critical Care, and Mother and Child Care services.

Do you provide joint replacement and spine care services?

Yes. The Orthopedics and Spine team at Hope Hospital provides evaluation and treatment for arthritis, trauma, sports injuries, deformity correction, total knee replacement, total hip replacement, and spine disorders.

Are specialist consultations available for heart and cancer care?

Yes. Hope Hospital offers specialist consultation pathways for cardiology and oncology with diagnosis, treatment planning, and follow-up support at associated departments and partner units.

How can I choose the right department before visiting?

Use the departments page to identify your condition area, then call 1800-233-0000 for guidance or appointment support. The team can route you to the correct specialist based on symptoms and urgency.

Need Complete Hospital Care?

For full hospital services, emergency support, and specialist access, start from our main hospital page.
