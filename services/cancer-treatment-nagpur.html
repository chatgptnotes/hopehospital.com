Cancer Surgery & Surgical Oncology in Nagpur

Hope Hospital Nagpur's Oncology Department provides comprehensive cancer care for patients from Nagpur, Vidarbha, and Central India. Our team of specialist oncology surgeons, medical oncologists, and oncology nurses deliver evidence-based cancer treatment tailored to each patient's diagnosis, stage, and overall health.

We focus on surgical oncology — the complete removal of tumors with curative intent using advanced minimally invasive and open surgical techniques. Our approach integrates surgery, chemotherapy, and where appropriate, radiation therapy referrals, ensuring every patient receives coordinated, multimodal cancer care.

As an Ayushman Bharat empaneled hospital, Hope Hospital provides cancer treatment completely free of cost for eligible patients under PM-JAY, MJPJAY, and other government schemes — removing financial barriers to cancer care for families across Maharashtra.

Warning Signs — When to See an Oncologist

  • Unexplained weight loss of more than 5 kg in 3 months
  • A lump or swelling anywhere in the body that does not go away
  • Change in bowel or bladder habits lasting more than 2–3 weeks
  • Blood in stool, urine, or sputum
  • Non-healing sores or ulcers in the mouth or on the skin
  • Persistent fatigue, fever, or night sweats without explanation
  • Difficulty swallowing or persistent hoarseness of voice

Free Cancer Treatment Under Ayushman Bharat in Nagpur

Hope Hospital is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY (Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana), CGHS, and ECHS. A wide range of cancer surgeries and chemotherapy protocols are covered under these schemes — eligible patients pay ₹0.

To avail free cancer treatment:

  • Visit Hope Hospital with your Ayushman Bharat / Golden Card (for PM-JAY) or MJPJAY card
  • Our empanelment desk will verify your eligibility within minutes
  • Bring all diagnosis reports, biopsy results, and prior treatment records
  • The hospital takes care of all scheme paperwork — no out-of-pocket payment for covered procedures

Frequently Asked Questions — Cancer Treatment Nagpur

Which is the best cancer hospital in Nagpur? +

Hope Hospital Nagpur is among the best cancer hospitals in Nagpur, offering comprehensive surgical oncology and multimodal cancer care at its NABH-accredited facility on Kamptee Road. It is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat, making cancer treatment accessible to all patients.

Is cancer treatment free under Ayushman Bharat in Nagpur? +

Yes. Hope Hospital is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) and MJPJAY. Many cancer surgeries and chemotherapy protocols are fully covered. Eligible patients pay ₹0. Bring your Ayushman / Golden Card to the hospital for cashless treatment.

What types of cancer are treated at Hope Hospital Nagpur? +

Hope Hospital treats colorectal cancer, gastrointestinal cancers (stomach, esophagus, liver, gallbladder, pancreas), breast cancer, oral cancer, thyroid cancer, and soft tissue sarcomas. The department specializes in surgical oncology with curative tumor removal.

Does Hope Hospital provide chemotherapy in Nagpur? +

Yes. Chemotherapy is administered in a supervised oncology ward. Our team coordinates pre-surgery (neoadjuvant) and post-surgery (adjuvant) chemotherapy as part of a comprehensive treatment plan for each cancer patient.
