Kidney Care in Nagpur — Overview

Hope Hospital Nagpur is a comprehensive kidney care center offering the full spectrum of nephrology services — from early CKD management and dialysis to kidney transplant surgery. As a NABH-accredited super specialty hospital, we provide international-standard kidney care without the need to travel to metro cities like Mumbai or Pune.

Our nephrology unit operates in partnership with NephroPlus, India's largest dialysis chain, ensuring consistent, high-quality dialysis with infection-controlled bays, high-flux dialyzers, and RO+UV purified water systems. Our transplant program handles both living-donor and cadaver (deceased-donor) kidney transplants, including all legal clearances through ZTCC Maharashtra.

Hope Hospital is fully empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, ECHS, and 20+ government health schemes. Eligible patients receive kidney transplant and dialysis services free of cost.

Kidney Transplant

End-to-end transplant program covering recipient evaluation, living-related and cadaver donor workup, HLA matching, crossmatch testing, transplant surgery, and lifelong post-transplant immunosuppression management. Transplant coordinators handle ZTCC legal clearances.

Hemodialysis (NephroPlus)

Thrice-weekly hemodialysis sessions (3–4 hours each) at our NephroPlus center. High-flux and low-flux dialyzer options, online HDF available, trained technicians, on-call nephrologist, strict infection control. Comfortable recliner stations with individual LCD screens.

monitor_heart

CRRT for ICU Patients

Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT) and SLED (Sustained Low-Efficiency Dialysis) for critically ill patients with acute kidney injury (AKI) in the ICU. Integrated with our 30-bed ICU for hemodynamically unstable patients who cannot tolerate conventional dialysis.

grain

Kidney Stone Treatment

Full range of stone management: ESWL (shock wave lithotripsy) for small-medium stones, ureteroscopy with holmium laser (URSL), PCNL (percutaneous nephrolithotomy) for large staghorn calculi, and RIRS (retrograde intrarenal surgery). Metabolic workup for recurrent stone formers.

medical_services

CKD Management

Long-term management of Chronic Kidney Disease stages 1–5 — blood pressure optimization, anemia correction with erythropoietin, bone mineral metabolism, dietary counselling, and timely preparation for dialysis or transplant when GFR declines below 15 ml/min.

Urological Surgery

Endoscopic and laparoscopic treatment for benign prostatic hyperplasia (TURP, laser prostatectomy), bladder tumors (TURBT), ureteric strictures, hydrocele, varicocele, and urinary tract reconstruction. Minimally invasive approach for faster recovery.

What are the warning signs of kidney disease?

Kidney disease is often silent in early stages. Do not ignore these warning signs — consult our nephrologist immediately if you notice:

warning Seek Medical Attention If You Have:

  • Swelling in feet, ankles, or around the eyes
  • Reduced urine output or dark / foamy urine
  • Persistent fatigue, weakness, or breathlessness
  • High blood pressure that is difficult to control
  • Nausea, loss of appetite, or unexplained weight loss
  • Muscle cramps or restless leg syndrome at night
  • Itching or dry skin without obvious cause
  • Confusion or difficulty concentrating (uremic encephalopathy)

Early detection of CKD can slow progression and delay the need for dialysis or transplant. Call 0712-2980073 to book a nephrology consultation.

Frequently Asked Questions — Kidney Treatment Nagpur

What is the cost of kidney transplant in Nagpur? +

Kidney transplant in Nagpur costs approximately ₹5 lakh to ₹8 lakh for a living-donor transplant and ₹6–₹9 lakh for a cadaver transplant, depending on immunosuppression protocol and hospital stay. Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, and ECHS patients receive it completely free. Call 0712-2980073 for a detailed estimate.

Is kidney transplant covered under Ayushman Bharat in Nagpur? +

Yes. Hope Hospital is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, ECHS, and 20+ government schemes. Kidney transplant surgery and related pre/post-operative care are covered for eligible patients. Bring your Ayushman / Golden Card to the hospital for verification.

What dialysis services are available at Hope Hospital Nagpur? +

Hope Hospital operates a NephroPlus dialysis center on campus with hemodialysis, online HDF, CRRT for ICU patients, and SLED. Multiple shifts are available daily (morning, afternoon, evening). The center uses high-flux dialyzers and RO+UV purified water exceeding AAMI standards. It is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat and major insurance TPAs.

What is NephroPlus dialysis and why is it preferred? +

NephroPlus is India's largest dialysis chain, known for standardized infection-control protocols, consistent quality across all centers, and trained technicians supervised by nephrologists. Hope Hospital's on-campus NephroPlus center combines hospital-grade emergency backup with the reliability of a specialized network — making it one of the safest dialysis centers in Nagpur.

How many dialysis sessions per week does a kidney patient need? +

Most end-stage renal disease (ESRD) patients require 3 hemodialysis sessions per week, each lasting 3–4 hours. Your nephrologist at Hope Hospital will prescribe the exact schedule based on your residual kidney function, weight, and blood test results. Some patients on home peritoneal dialysis (CAPD) do 4–5 exchanges daily.

What are the warning signs of kidney failure? +

Warning signs include: reduced urine output, swelling in feet/ankles/eyes, persistent fatigue, difficulty breathing, nausea and loss of appetite, high blood pressure, foamy or dark urine, and confusion. These symptoms require immediate medical evaluation — call 0712-2980073 for emergency assessment.

What kidney stone treatments are available in Nagpur? +

Hope Hospital offers ESWL (shock wave lithotripsy) for small to medium stones, ureteroscopy with holmium laser (URSL) for ureteric stones, PCNL (percutaneous nephrolithotomy) for large or staghorn calculi, and RIRS (retrograde intrarenal surgery) for renal pelvis stones. The right procedure is selected based on stone size, location, and composition.

Can I get a living donor kidney transplant in Nagpur? +

Yes. Hope Hospital's transplant program accepts living-related donors — spouse, siblings, parents, and children are eligible. The program includes complete donor evaluation, HLA and crossmatch testing, and ZTCC Maharashtra legal clearance. Cadaver (deceased donor) transplants from the government's organ donation waitlist are also available through ZTCC empanelment.
