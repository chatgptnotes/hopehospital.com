Neurosurgery in Nagpur — Overview

Hope Hospital Nagpur's Neurosurgery Department is the region's leading center for brain and spine surgery, offering the full spectrum of neurosurgical care — from emergency neurotrauma and brain tumor removal to complex spine reconstruction and epilepsy surgery. Patients from Nagpur, Vidarbha, and across Central India choose Hope Hospital for conditions that previously required travel to Mumbai, Pune, or Hyderabad.

Our neurosurgery team operates using advanced neuronavigation, intraoperative imaging, and microscopic surgical techniques that enable precise, minimally invasive procedures with shorter hospital stays and faster recovery. A dedicated 30-bed ICU with 24/7 neuro-intensivist cover ensures safe postoperative care for all neurosurgical patients.

Hope Hospital is fully empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, ECHS, and 20+ government schemes. Eligible patients receive brain and spine surgery free of cost.

What neurosurgery procedures does Hope Hospital offer?

psychology

Brain Tumor Surgery

Craniotomy for gliomas, meningiomas, acoustic neuromas, pituitary tumors, and metastatic brain lesions. Neuronavigation-guided resection maximizes tumor removal while protecting eloquent brain areas. Endoscopic approaches for ventricular and skull base tumors minimize incision and recovery time.

Spine Surgery

Cervical and lumbar discectomy for slipped disc (PIVD), spinal canal decompression (laminectomy), spinal fusion for spondylolisthesis and instability, vertebroplasty and kyphoplasty for compression fractures, and minimally invasive spine surgery (MISS) for faster recovery and less blood loss.

emergency

Neurotrauma & Head Injury

Emergency craniotomy for extradural hematoma (EDH), subdural hematoma (SDH), intracerebral hemorrhage (ICH), and depressed skull fractures from road traffic accidents and falls. Decompressive craniectomy for severe traumatic brain injury (TBI) with brain swelling.

water_drop

Neurovascular Surgery

Surgical clipping and endovascular coiling for brain aneurysms, surgical resection of arteriovenous malformations (AVM), carotid endarterectomy for stroke prevention, and mechanical thrombectomy with IV thrombolysis for acute ischemic stroke within the treatment window.

bolt

Epilepsy Surgery

Surgical evaluation and treatment for drug-resistant epilepsy — temporal lobectomy, lesionectomy for focal cortical dysplasia, and corpus callosotomy. Pre-surgical workup includes video EEG monitoring, MRI epilepsy protocol (3T), PET scan, and neuropsychological assessment before any surgical decision.

Peripheral Nerve Surgery

Carpal tunnel release, ulnar nerve decompression at the elbow, brachial plexus injury repair, nerve grafting for traumatic nerve injuries, and microvascular nerve repair. Also covers tarsal tunnel syndrome, peroneal nerve palsy, and thoracic outlet syndrome surgery.

What are the warning signs you need a neurosurgeon?

Many brain and spine conditions deteriorate rapidly if left untreated. Seek immediate evaluation if you or a family member has any of the following:

warning Seek Emergency Care Immediately For:

  • Sudden severe headache ("thunderclap headache" — worst headache of your life)
  • Weakness or numbness on one side of the body, face, arm, or leg
  • Sudden confusion, difficulty speaking, or not understanding speech
  • Loss of consciousness or unresponsiveness after a head injury
  • New-onset seizures in an adult
  • Sudden vision loss in one or both eyes
  • Severe neck pain with high fever (meningitis)
  • Loss of bladder/bowel control with back pain (spinal emergency)

Frequently Asked Questions — Neurosurgery Nagpur

What is the cost of brain tumor surgery in Nagpur? +

Brain tumor surgery (craniotomy) in Nagpur costs approximately ₹2 lakh to ₹5 lakh depending on tumor type, size, location, and approach. Endoscopic procedures for pituitary or ventricular tumors may cost ₹1.5–₹3.5 lakh. Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, and ECHS patients receive it free. Is brain surgery covered under Ayushman Bharat in Nagpur? +

Yes. Hope Hospital is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, and ECHS. Brain tumor surgery, spine surgery, emergency craniotomy for head injuries, and neurovascular procedures are covered for eligible patients. Bring your Ayushman / Golden Card to the hospital for verification — no advance payment is required for eligible procedures.

What spine surgeries are performed at Hope Hospital Nagpur? +

Hope Hospital's neurosurgery team performs cervical discectomy and fusion (ACDF) for neck disc problems, lumbar microdiscectomy for slipped disc causing sciatica, spinal canal decompression (laminectomy), PLIF/TLIF spinal fusion for spondylolisthesis and instability, vertebroplasty/kyphoplasty for osteoporotic compression fractures, and minimally invasive spine surgery (MISS) with smaller incisions and faster recovery.

What are the warning signs of a brain tumor? +

Warning signs include persistent or progressively worsening headaches (often worse in the morning), new-onset seizures, gradual weakness or numbness in arms or legs, vision changes or double vision, difficulty speaking or understanding, personality or behavioral changes, and unexplained nausea with vomiting. If you have any of these symptoms, call 0712-2980073 to arrange an urgent MRI brain and neurosurgery consultation.

How is a brain stroke treated at Hope Hospital Nagpur? +

Ischemic stroke (clot blocking a brain artery) is treated with IV thrombolysis (alteplase) within 4.5 hours of symptom onset, and mechanical thrombectomy for large vessel occlusions within 24 hours. Hemorrhagic stroke (bleeding in or around the brain) may require emergency craniotomy to evacuate the clot, or endovascular coiling for a ruptured brain aneurysm. Hope Hospital has 24/7 emergency neurosurgery and CT angiography capability for stroke response.

What is the recovery time after spine surgery? +

After minimally invasive spine surgery (MISS) or microdiscectomy, most patients are walking the next day and return to light work in 2–4 weeks. Open spinal fusion (PLIF/TLIF) typically requires 6 weeks to 3 months before return to work, and 6–12 months for full bone fusion. Physical therapy after spine surgery significantly speeds recovery. Your neurosurgeon will give a specific timeline based on the procedure performed.

Does Hope Hospital treat head injuries from road accidents? +

Yes. Hope Hospital has a 24/7 neurotrauma emergency pathway for road traffic accidents. Emergency CT scan is available round-the-clock. Our neurosurgery team manages extradural hematomas (EDH), subdural hematomas (SDH), intracerebral contusions, and depressed skull fractures with emergency craniotomy when required. The 30-bed ICU provides post-operative neuro-intensive care. Call 0712-2980073 for immediate emergency response.

Is epilepsy surgery available in Nagpur? +

Yes. For patients whose seizures are not controlled by 2 or more anti-epileptic drugs (drug-resistant epilepsy), surgical evaluation is offered at Hope Hospital. Pre-surgical workup includes prolonged video-EEG monitoring, high-resolution MRI epilepsy protocol, FDG-PET scan, and neuropsychological testing. Surgical options include temporal lobectomy, lesionectomy for focal cortical dysplasia, and corpus callosotomy for drop attacks. A multidisciplinary epilepsy team makes the decision collaboratively.
