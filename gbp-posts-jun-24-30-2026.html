Kidney Stones Spike in Summer — Spot the Signs

Saturday, 27 June 2026 · Health Tip / Nephrology & Urology
⬜ TO POST
Post Text
Long, hot Nagpur summers + not enough water = the perfect recipe for kidney stones. Watch for these signs: 🔴 Sharp, gripping pain in the side or lower back 🔴 Pain that radiates to the lower abdomen or groin 🔴 Burning, or pink/red urine 🔴 Nausea — or fever with chills (this one is urgent) The good news: most stones today are removed without open surgery. Modern laser procedures like RIRS need no cuts and are often a same-day treatment. At Hope Hospital, urologist Dr. Jayant Nikose and our kidney-care team treat stones with RIRS, PCNL and ESWL — cashless for eligible cardholders under Ayushman Bharat. 💧 Drink 3+ litres of water a day this summer. 📍 Teka Naka, Kamptee Road, Nagpur – 440026 #KidneyStones #RIRS #KidneyStoneTreatment #UrologistNagpur #DrJayantNikose #HopeHospitalNagpur #SummerHealth #NABHAccredited #NagpurHealth #KampteeRoad

Heart Attack: Every Minute Counts

Monday, 29 June 2026 · Health Tip / Cardiac (Zion)
⬜ TO POST
Post Text
In a heart attack, the first 60 minutes — the "golden hour" — decide everything. Know the warning signs: ❤️ Chest pain, pressure or tightness (may spread to the arm, jaw or back) ❤️ Sudden breathlessness or a cold sweat ❤️ Nausea, dizziness or unusual fatigue Don't wait it out. Don't drive yourself. Get to a hospital with a 24/7 cath lab immediately. Hope Hospital's Zion Cardiac Centre offers round-the-clock emergency cardiac care — cath lab, angioplasty and ICU — covered cashless for eligible cardholders under Ayushman Bharat.
