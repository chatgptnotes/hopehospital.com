Summer Kidney Stones — Prevention & Free RIRS

Tuesday, 2 June 2026 · Seasonal + Service Highlight
Post Text
💧 Nagpur summer alert: Kidney stones spike 30% in May-June The cause is simple: heat + low water intake = concentrated urine + crystallized minerals. Anyone working outdoors, drivers, or those who don't drink enough water are at risk. Symptoms to act on immediately: 🔴 Sudden severe pain in lower back/side (radiates to groin) 🔴 Blood in urine 🔴 Painful urination 🔴 Fever with pain — go to ER Modern treatment is fast & free: ✓ RIRS laser surgery (no skin cut, same-day discharge) ✓ ESWL shock-wave lithotripsy for small stones ✓ All covered under Ayushman Bharat for eligible cardholders at Hope Hospital ✓ Partnership with NephroPlus dialysis network Prevention: 3-4 litres water daily, lemon water twice a day, reduce salt. 🌐 Full guide: hopehospital.com/blog/rirs-surgery-nagpur-kidney-stones-2026

World Food Safety Day — Safe Food, Healthy Gut

Sunday, 7 June 2026 · Awareness Day + Gastroenterology
Post Text
🍽️ World Food Safety Day — June 7 Summer + monsoon transition = peak food-poisoning season in India. Contaminated water, street food, undercooked meat, and dairy left out are top culprits. 5 simple food safety rules for monsoon: 1. Drink only filtered or boiled water — even ice cubes 2. Avoid pre-cut fruits from street vendors 3. Eat hot, freshly-cooked food (avoid cold buffets) 4. Wash hands before eating 5. Refrigerate leftovers within 2 hours When to see a gastroenterologist: 🔴 Diarrhea lasting >2 days 🔴 Blood in stool 🔴 Persistent vomiting (can't keep water down) 🔴 High fever 🔴 Severe abdominal pain 🔴 Dehydration signs (dry mouth, dizziness, reduced urine) Hope Hospital's gastroenterology team treats: ✓ Acute gastroenteritis / food poisoning ✓ Endoscopy & colonoscopy ✓ Gallbladder stones (laparoscopic surgery — Ayushman covered) ✓ Liver disease ✓ Chronic acidity & reflux Led by Dr. Nikhil Khobragade (Gastroenterologist). 🌐 hopehospital.com/departments/gastroenterology
