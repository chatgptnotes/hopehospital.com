Kidney Warning Signs

Thursday, 18 June 2026 · Health Tip
✅ POSTED
Post Text
Your kidneys rarely complain — until the damage is done. 🚨 Warning signs you shouldn't ignore: ▪ Swelling in feet, ankles or face ▪ Foamy or blood-tinged urine ▪ Uncontrolled high blood pressure ▪ Rising creatinine on blood tests ▪ Persistent fatigue or loss of appetite If you live with diabetes or hypertension, a simple kidney function test is wise. Early care can slow kidney disease significantly. Hope Hospital's nephrology team is here to help, Kamptee Road, Nagpur. 📍 Teka Naka, Nagpur, Maharashtra 440026 #KidneyHealth #KidneyDisease #NephrologistNagpur #HealthTips #CKD #HopeHospitalNagpur #NagpurHealth #KidneyWarning #KampteeRoad #PreventiveCare
