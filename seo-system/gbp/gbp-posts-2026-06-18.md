**Headline:** Kidney Warning Signs You Shouldn't Ignore

**Body:**
Your kidneys often struggle silently — early symptoms are easy to miss. Watch for:
• Swelling in the feet, ankles or around the eyes
• Constant tiredness or trouble concentrating
• Changes in urination — foamy, bloody, or more/less than usual
• Persistent itching, nausea or loss of appetite
• High blood pressure that's hard to control
