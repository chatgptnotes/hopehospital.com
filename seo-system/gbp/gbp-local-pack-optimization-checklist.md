by your **Google Business Profile**, not homepage HTML. This is the single highest-ROI lever for the head term.

The local pack is ranked on **Relevance · Distance · Prominence.** You can't change distance — so maximize
relevance (categories, services, content) and prominence (reviews, citations, links).

## What moves the needle (priority order)

- ❌ Fake/incentivized reviews or self-reviews.
- ❌ Categories for services you don't provide.
- ❌ A second/duplicate GBP listing (consolidate the "Ayushman Nagpur Hospital" 656-review listing question first — confirm if it's a separate real entity or a duplicate to merge).
