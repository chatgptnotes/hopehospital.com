# Reviews Generation System — Hope Hospital

**Goal:** lift the Google rating from **3.9★ (415 reviews)** toward **4.3★+**, the band where
competitors (Wockhardt, Max, NewEra: 4.3–4.5) sit and where Google's local pack and AI
assistants start surfacing you. This is the single highest-impact local-SEO lever.

> **Why it matters more than the website:** the ChatGPT "best hospital in Nagpur" answer and
> the Google Map Pack both rank by rating + review volume + velocity. On-page SEO can't move
> this — only a steady flow of genuine 4–5★ reviews can.

## The one link to use everywhere
**Leave-a-review link:** https://g.page/r/CXNeWp4Fw2cOEBM/review
- Shorten it (e.g. bit.ly/hope-review) for SMS, and print it as a **QR code** on:
  discharge summaries, billing counter, reception desk, OPD rooms, pharmacy counter.

## The process (make it a daily habit, not a one-off campaign)
1. **Ask at discharge / after a positive OPD visit.** Front desk: "If we cared for you well,
   a 1-minute Google review really helps other patients find us — here's the QR." A verbal ask
   from staff is the #1 driver of reviews.
2. **Follow up within 24h by WhatsApp** (official line **9823555053**) with the link. Same-day
   requests convert far better than later ones.
3. **Filter unhappy patients first.** Before asking for a public review, ask "How was your
   experience?" If unhappy, route to a private feedback contact and resolve it — do NOT push
   them to Google. This protects the rating from avoidable 1-stars.
4. **Reply to every review within 48h** (templates: see `gbp-optimization-and-posts.md`).
   Replying signals an active business to Google and to prospective patients.

## Volume target (realistic)
At ~415 reviews / 3.9★, you lift the average fastest with **consistent new 5★ reviews**:
- **Target: 15–25 new genuine reviews/week.** At that pace the visible average climbs over
  6–10 weeks (older low reviews get diluted).
- Track weekly in `seo-system/reports/` alongside the SEO KPIs.

## Do / Don't
- ✅ Ask genuinely satisfied patients; make it effortless (QR + same-day link).
- ✅ Reply to all reviews, especially negative ones, calmly and helpfully.
- ❌ Never offer money/discounts for reviews, never post fake reviews, never bulk-request in one
  day (review spikes look unnatural to Google). Steady > spiky.
- ❌ Don't ask dissatisfied patients for a public review — resolve privately first.
