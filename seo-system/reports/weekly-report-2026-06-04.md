## 🎯 Top opportunities (concrete next actions)
1. **"hope hospital" (brand variant)** — 1,106 impressions but only **0.9% CTR at position 8.4**. Biggest single pool of impressions; we rank 8th for a variant of our own name because other "Hope Hospital" entities elsewhere outrank us. *Slow climb — keep strengthening brand/NAP signals.*
2. **Nephrology** — position 9.7, 356 impressions, 0 clicks. On the edge of page 1. *Action: named-specialist (E-E-A-T) work — see plan.*
3. **Contact page** — 1,524 impressions, 0.9% CTR. Looks alarming but is partly *by design*: the snippet already shows the phone number, so many people call without clicking. Not a true problem.
4. **"ayushman bharat package list pdf 2026"** — position 8.8. People want the downloadable PDF; the Ayushman blog already targets this and should climb.

## 🔧 What we changed this period (all live)
- **Stopped pages competing with each other** (keyword cannibalization) on **cancer, neurosurgery, knee, and kidney**, and removed a **duplicate homepage clone** (`/nagpur`) that was splitting the "best hospital in Nagpur" signal.
- **AI-search readiness (GEO):** reworded 19 headings across 7 treatment pages into question form so ChatGPT / Gemini / Perplexity can quote them.
- **Trust & accuracy:** softened unqualified "FREE under Ayushman" wording to "covered for eligible cardholders"; removed an inaccurate "NABH since 2005" claim; synced the review count to the live **3.9★ / 415 reviews**.
- **New content:** *Free Cancer Treatment under Ayushman* and *RIRS Kidney-Stone* blogs.

**What to expect:** ranking changes take **~2–3 weeks** to appear — none of this shows in the numbers above yet. Re-measure around **25 June**.
