About Dr. B.K. Murali

Dr. B.K. Murali is widely regarded as the best orthopedic surgeon in Nagpur and one of Central India's most respected joint replacement specialists. With over 19 years of experience and an M.S. (Ortho) qualification, he has transformed the orthopedic care landscape in Vidarbha by bringing advanced, international-standard surgical techniques to patients in the region.

As the founder and medical director of Hope Hospital — a NABH-accredited 200-bed super-specialty hospital at Teka Naka, Kamptee Road, Nagpur — Dr. Murali has built one of the region's most comprehensive orthopedic and multi-specialty care centers. Under his leadership, Hope Hospital has performed thousands of successful joint replacement, spine, and arthroscopy surgeries.

Beyond clinical work, Dr. Murali is also a healthcare innovator. He developed DrMHope, an AI-powered healthcare software platform that integrates clinical decision support, patient management, and real-time diagnostics. This work made him a finalist in India's Innovation Growth Programme — a prestigious joint initiative of the Department of Science & Technology (DST), FICCI, and the Indo-US Science & Technology Forum.

Areas of Specialization

accessible Total Knee Replacement

Minimally invasive and computer-navigated total and partial knee replacement using high-quality implants. Ideal for severe arthritis, knee degeneration, and failed conservative treatment.

accessibility_new Total Hip Replacement

Total hip arthroplasty, bipolar hemiarthroplasty, and revision hip surgery using anterior and posterior approaches tailored to each patient's anatomy and activity goals.

biotech Arthroscopy Surgery

Keyhole surgery for knee, shoulder, ankle, and elbow — including ACL/PCL reconstruction, meniscus repair, rotator cuff repair, and diagnostic arthroscopy with rapid recovery.

straighten Spine Surgery

Microscope-assisted and minimally invasive spine procedures: disc replacement, spinal fusion, laminectomy, vertebroplasty, and treatment for scoliosis and spinal stenosis.

sports_martial_arts Sports Medicine

Management of sports injuries including ligament tears, tendon damage, stress fractures, and overuse injuries with structured return-to-sport rehabilitation programs.

emergency Trauma & Fracture Care

24/7 emergency orthopedic trauma services — complex fractures, dislocations, and polytrauma managed with intramedullary nails, plates, external fixators, and Ilizarov techniques.
