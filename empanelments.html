Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hope Hospital empaneled under Ayushman Bharat and government schemes?

Yes. Hope Hospital supports multiple government schemes including Ayushman Bharat and other empanelment programs, helping eligible patients access cashless or approved treatment pathways.

Which insurance and TPA services are accepted at Hope Hospital?

Hope Hospital is associated with major insurance providers and TPAs. Coverage and approval can vary by plan, so patients should confirm policy eligibility and required documents before admission.

What documents are usually required for cashless treatment?

Patients generally need government ID, policy card or e-card, referral or authorization letter (if applicable), and treating doctor advice. The billing desk can confirm the exact checklist.

How can I verify if my treatment is covered under my scheme?

Share your scheme details with the empanelment or billing team before admission. They will validate eligibility, package mapping, and pre-authorization requirements with the insurer or scheme portal.
