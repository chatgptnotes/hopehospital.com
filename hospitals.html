Frequently Asked Questions

Where are Hope Hospital facilities located?

Hope Hospital has facilities at Teka Naka, Ramdaspeth, and Pandhurna coverage points to support patients across Nagpur and nearby regions, with emergency and specialist services available through the network.

Which is the main hospital branch for emergency care?

The Teka Naka branch is the main super specialty center and is equipped for 24x7 emergency response, ICU care, advanced diagnostics, and surgical support.

Are all branches open every day?

Emergency support is available 24x7, while consultation timing may vary by branch and specialty. Patients are advised to call before visiting for same-day OPD slot confirmation.

How do I decide which branch to visit first?

For emergencies, visit the nearest Hope Hospital branch immediately. For planned consultations and procedures, call 1800-233-0000 so the team can direct you to the best location and specialist.
