About Hope Hospital – Nagpur's NABH Accredited Super Specialty Center

Best Hospital in Nagpur - NABH Accredited Excellence Since 2012

Hope Hospital stands as the best hospital in Nagpur and Central India's first NABH-accredited comprehensive super specialty center, providing exceptional medical treatment to over 5 million patients within a 500 km radius of Nagpur.

Led by Dr. B.K. Murali, the best orthopedic surgeon in Nagpur, our team of 20+ specialist doctors delivers expert treatment for orthopedic surgery, neurosurgery, cardiology, and all super specialty services — across our 100-bed, 24,000 sq ft NABH-accredited facility.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Hope Hospital one of the leading hospitals in Nagpur?

Hope Hospital is a NABH-accredited super specialty center in Nagpur with 24x7 emergency care, 200+ beds, ICU support, modular operation theaters, and multi-specialty consultants across orthopedics, cardiology, oncology, nephrology, neurology, and critical care.

Who is Dr. B.K. Murali at Hope Hospital?

Dr. B.K. Murali is an orthopedic and joint replacement specialist associated with Hope Hospital, known for expertise in knee replacement, hip replacement, spine surgery, arthroscopy, and trauma care.

How many branches does Hope Hospital operate?

Hope Hospital operates across key locations including Teka Naka (main hospital), Ramdaspeth, and Pandhurna outreach support, providing emergency and specialist consultation access for patients in and around Nagpur.

What major specialties are available at Hope Hospital?

Key specialties include Orthopedics and joint replacement, Cardiology, Oncology, Nephrology with dialysis support, Neurosciences, Gastroenterology, and Critical Care with emergency response.
