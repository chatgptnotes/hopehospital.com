Comprehensive Maternity & Gynecology Care in Nagpur

The Mother & Child Care department at NABH-accredited Hope Hospital Nagpur brings together specialist doctors — obstetricians, gynecologists, neonatologists, and pediatricians — under one roof. Our mission: healthy mothers, healthy babies, and families supported from pre-conception counselling through delivery treatment and postpartum recovery.

We understand that pregnancy is a deeply personal healthcare experience. That is why our specialist doctor team takes the time to listen, explain every medical option, and build a birth plan tailored to each patient's history and preferences. Whether you are expecting your first child or managing a high-risk pregnancy, Hope Hospital has the expertise, equipment, and empathy to guide you safely through every milestone.

Our Medical Services

pregnant_woman

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Full-spectrum obstetric care including prenatal checkups, ultrasound monitoring, gestational diabetes screening, and postnatal follow-up. Our gynecology wing handles menstrual disorders, PCOD/PCOS management, endometriosis, and minimally invasive hysterectomy.

child_care

Neonatal Care & NICU

State-of-the-art Neonatal Intensive Care Unit equipped with ventilators, radiant warmers, phototherapy, and continuous monitoring. Our neonatologists manage premature births, low birth-weight infants, respiratory distress, and neonatal infections round the clock.

vaccines

Pediatric Services

General pediatrics covering newborn screening, vaccination schedules, growth monitoring, and management of childhood illnesses such as asthma, allergies, and infections. Specialist referrals are coordinated in-house for complex cases.

hotel

Maternity Ward

Comfortable, private maternity rooms with attached washrooms, family-friendly visiting policies, and 24-hour nursing support. Our labour rooms are equipped with fetal monitors, emergency crash carts, and immediate access to the operation theatre.

monitor_heart

High-Risk Pregnancy Unit

Dedicated care for conditions such as pre-eclampsia, gestational diabetes, placenta previa, multiple pregnancies, and Rh-incompatibility. Continuous fetal monitoring, advanced Doppler ultrasonography, and an obstetric ICU are available on site.

medical_services

Normal & C-Section Delivery

We encourage natural childbirth whenever safe for both mother and baby, supported by labour analgesia (painless delivery) options. Caesarean sections are performed with modern surgical techniques, ensuring quick recovery and minimal complications.

Why Choose Hope Hospital for Maternity Care?

Hope Hospital is one of Nagpur's most trusted names in maternity and newborn care. Our integrated approach means that obstetricians, anesthesiologists, neonatologists, and nurses work as a single team -- communicating in real time so that no detail is missed during labour or delivery.

For high-risk cases we go further: a maternal-fetal medicine specialist doctor reviews each case, cardiologists and endocrinologists are available for medical consultation within the same campus, and our blood bank maintains emergency reserves around the clock. This level of care and preparedness at Hope Hospital has helped us maintain consistently strong maternal and neonatal outcomes year after year.

Beyond the clinical, we invest in the human side of care. Antenatal classes cover breathing exercises, breastfeeding guidance, and newborn care basics. New mothers receive lactation support before discharge. And every family leaves with a clear postpartum care plan and a direct line to our pediatric helpdesk.

Facilities & Infrastructure

Our maternity wing is designed with safety and comfort in mind. Key facilities include:

  • Fully equipped NICU with Level II+ capabilities
  • Dedicated obstetric operation theatre with immediate neonatal resuscitation setup
  • 24/7 in-house obstetrician, anesthesiologist, and neonatologist availability
  • Advanced fetal monitoring and Doppler ultrasound
  • In-house blood bank and pathology lab for emergency cross-match
  • Private maternity suites with family stay option
  • Breastfeeding support and lactation counselling rooms

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline Does Hope Hospital have a NICU for premature babies?

Yes. Hope Hospital operates a fully equipped Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) with ventilators, warmers, phototherapy units, and round-the-clock neonatologist coverage. Our NICU is capable of managing premature births from 28 weeks onward, as well as neonates requiring critical interventions such as respiratory support, IV nutrition, and infection management.

help_outline What high-risk pregnancy services are available at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Our high-risk pregnancy unit provides specialized care for gestational diabetes, pre-eclampsia, placenta previa, multiple pregnancies, and Rh-incompatibility. We offer continuous fetal monitoring, advanced ultrasonography including Doppler studies, and a dedicated obstetric ICU. Our senior gynecologists collaborate with cardiologists, endocrinologists, and intensivists for comprehensive maternal care.

help_outline Are normal deliveries encouraged at Hope Hospital?

Absolutely. Our obstetric team prioritizes natural childbirth wherever it is safe for mother and baby. We maintain one of the highest normal delivery rates in Nagpur. Caesarean sections are performed only when medically indicated. We provide labour analgesia (painless delivery), birth companionship, and immediate skin-to-skin contact to support a positive birthing experience.
