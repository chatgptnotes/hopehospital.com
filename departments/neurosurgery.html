About Our Neurosurgery Department in Nagpur

Hope Hospital’s Neurosurgery unit — operating under the Hope Neurology brand — is one of Nagpur’s most advanced brain and spine surgical centres. Dedicated neurosurgeons here manage the full spectrum of cranial, spinal, and peripheral nerve conditions: from brain tumours and cerebrovascular aneurysms to lumbar disc herniations, epilepsy, and spinal trauma. With 24/7 emergency neurosurgical availability and a NABH-accredited infrastructure, patients across Nagpur and Vidarbha receive expert, timely care without travelling to Mumbai or Pune.

We are equipped with a Carl Zeiss surgical microscope, one of the most advanced intraoperative visualization systems available, which allows our specialist doctor-surgeons to perform delicate brain and spine surgeries with exceptional precision. Combined with our modular operation theaters, C-arm flat panel imaging, and neuronavigation capabilities, our medical department delivers treatment outcomes that match the best neurosurgical centers in the country.

Every patient at our neurosurgery department receives a thorough diagnostic evaluation, a clearly explained treatment plan, and dedicated post-operative rehabilitation. Our approach is conservative first -- surgery is recommended only when it offers a clear advantage over non-surgical management. When surgery is indicated, we prioritize minimally invasive techniques wherever possible to reduce recovery time and improve patient comfort.

Conditions We Treat

Our neurosurgery department manages a wide spectrum of neurological conditions, from common to complex. Below are some of the key conditions our specialists treat on a regular basis:

  • Brain tumors (benign and malignant)
  • Traumatic brain injuries
  • Herniated disc (cervical, thoracic, lumbar)
  • Spinal stenosis
  • Hydrocephalus
  • Epilepsy and seizure disorders
  • Trigeminal neuralgia
  • Cerebral aneurysms
  • Spinal cord tumors
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Chiari malformation
  • Peripheral nerve injuries

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline What types of brain tumors are treated at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Hope Hospital's neurosurgery department treats all types of brain tumors including gliomas, meningiomas, pituitary adenomas, acoustic neuromas, metastatic brain tumors, and skull base tumors. Our neurosurgeons use advanced Carl Zeiss microscope-assisted techniques for precise tumor removal while preserving healthy brain tissue. Treatment plans are personalized based on tumor type, size, location, and the patient's overall health, often involving a multidisciplinary team approach with oncology support.

help_outline Is minimally invasive spine surgery available at Hope Hospital?

Yes, Hope Hospital offers minimally invasive spine neurosurgery procedures including microdiscectomy, endoscopic spine surgery, minimally invasive lumbar fusion, and percutaneous pedicle screw fixation. These advanced techniques result in smaller incisions, less blood loss, reduced post-operative pain, shorter hospital stays, and faster recovery compared to traditional open surgery. Our neurosurgeons are trained in the latest minimally invasive approaches for conditions such as herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and spinal instability.

help_outline Does Hope Hospital provide emergency neurosurgery services?

Yes, Hope Hospital provides 24/7 emergency neurosurgery services for conditions requiring urgent intervention, including traumatic brain injuries, epidural and subdural hematomas, subarachnoid hemorrhage, spinal cord injuries, and acute hydrocephalus. Our emergency department is equipped with advanced imaging facilities for rapid diagnosis, and our neurosurgical team is available round the clock. With 30 ICU beds and state-of-the-art monitoring equipment, we ensure comprehensive post-operative care for all emergency neurosurgery patients.

help_outline What neurological conditions does the neurosurgery department at Hope Hospital treat?

Hope Hospital's neurosurgery department treats a wide range of conditions including brain tumors, spinal disc herniation, lumbar spondylosis, cervical myelopathy, epilepsy, hydrocephalus, Parkinson's disease, head trauma, cerebral aneurysms, arteriovenous malformations (AVMs), peripheral nerve injuries, and carpal tunnel syndrome. Post-surgical neurological rehabilitation is available on-site to support complete recovery.

help_outline What is the cost of brain surgery and spine surgery in Nagpur at Hope Hospital?

Neurosurgery costs vary based on the procedure, complexity, and hospital stay. Patients covered under Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY or MJPJAY can avail major brain and spine surgeries completely free at Hope Hospital, which is empaneled under both schemes. For patients without government coverage, the hospital offers transparent pricing. Call 071-22980073 for a procedure-specific estimate.

help_outline How long is recovery after spine surgery at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

For minimally invasive procedures like microdiscectomy, patients are typically mobilised within 24 hours and discharged in 2–3 days, returning to light activity in 2–4 weeks. Open spinal fusion requires a 4–7 day stay with full recovery in 6–12 weeks. Our physiotherapy and neurological rehabilitation team creates a personalised recovery plan for each patient.

help_outline Is neurosurgery available free under Ayushman Bharat at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Yes, Hope Hospital is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY and MJPJAY. A wide range of neurosurgery procedures — including brain tumor surgery, spinal surgery, and head trauma procedures — are covered for eligible beneficiaries. Bring your Ayushman Bharat card to the hospital's empanelment desk for eligibility verification. Call 071-22980073 to confirm coverage for your specific procedure.
