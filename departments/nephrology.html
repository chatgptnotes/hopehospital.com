Nephrology & Urology – Kidney Specialists in Nagpur

Comprehensive kidney and urinary tract care -- from dialysis and transplant to kidney stone treatment and chronic kidney disease management -- powered by our NephroPlus partnership.

Complete Kidney & Urological Care in Nagpur

The Nephrology and Urology department at NABH-accredited Hope Hospital Nagpur provides integrated treatment for all conditions affecting the kidneys, ureters, bladder, and urinary tract. Our specialist nephrologists and urologists work together to deliver medical and surgical solutions that preserve kidney function, manage end-stage renal disease, and treat urological conditions with the latest techniques.

verified NephroPlus Dialysis Partner -- India's Largest Dialysis Network

Through our partnership with NephroPlus, patients at Hope Hospital Nagpur have access to specialist healthcare dialysis services that meet international quality standards. NephroPlus brings standardized protocols, advanced water treatment, and trained medical technicians to ensure every dialysis session is safe, comfortable, and effective for every patient.

Our Medical Services & Treatments

water_drop

Dialysis Services

On-campus NephroPlus dialysis center offering hemodialysis with high-flux dialyzers, CRRT (Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy) for critically ill patients, and SLED (Sustained Low-Efficiency Dialysis). Multiple shifts daily with comfortable recliner stations, individual LCD screens, and infection-controlled bays.

swap_horiz

Kidney Transplant

End-to-end transplant program covering recipient evaluation, living-related and cadaver donor workup, HLA matching, crossmatch testing, transplant surgery, and lifelong post-transplant immunosuppression management. Our transplant coordinators handle legal and institutional clearances.

grain

Kidney Stone Treatment

Full range of stone management: ESWL (shock wave lithotripsy), ureteroscopy with holmium laser, PCNL (percutaneous nephrolithotomy) for large calculi, and RIRS (retrograde intrarenal surgery). Metabolic workup for recurrent stone formers to prevent future episodes.

medical_services

Urological Surgery

Surgical treatment for benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) including TURP and laser prostatectomy, bladder tumors (TURBT), ureteric strictures, hydrocele, varicocele, and urinary incontinence. Most procedures are performed endoscopically or laparoscopically.

monitor_heart

Chronic Kidney Disease Management

Long-term management of CKD stages 1-5, including blood pressure optimization, anemia correction with erythropoietin, bone mineral metabolism management, dietary counselling, and timely preparation for dialysis or transplant when kidney function declines.

NephroPlus Dialysis Center at Hope Hospital

Our NephroPlus dialysis unit operates within the Hope Hospital campus, combining the convenience of hospital-based care with the expertise of India's largest dialysis chain. Key features include:

  • RO + UV treated water system exceeding AAMI standards
  • High-flux and low-flux dialyzer options based on patient needs
  • Individual dialysis stations with recliner chairs for comfort
  • Trained dialysis technicians and on-call nephrologist at all times
  • Strict infection control protocols with hepatitis isolation bays
  • Empaneled under Ayushman Bharat, CGHS, ECHS, and major insurance TPAs
  • Multiple shifts (morning, afternoon, evening) for scheduling flexibility

When to See a Nephrologist

Many kidney diseases are silent in early stages. You should consult our nephrology team if you experience:

  • Persistent swelling of feet, ankles, or face
  • Foamy or blood-tinged urine
  • Elevated creatinine or reduced GFR on blood tests
  • Uncontrolled high blood pressure despite medication
  • Recurrent urinary tract infections or kidney stones
  • Diabetes or hypertension lasting more than 5 years (routine screening recommended)

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline What is the NephroPlus partnership at Hope Hospital?

Hope Hospital has partnered with NephroPlus, India's largest dialysis network, to operate a state-of-the-art dialysis center on campus. This partnership brings internationally standardized dialysis protocols, water purification systems (RO + UV), and trained dialysis technicians to Nagpur. The NephroPlus unit at Hope Hospital offers hemodialysis with high-flux dialyzers, continuous renal replacement therapy (CRRT) for ICU patients, and Sustained Low-Efficiency Dialysis (SLED). The center operates multiple shifts daily, making it convenient for patients requiring thrice-weekly dialysis.

help_outline Does Hope Hospital perform kidney transplant surgery?

Yes. Hope Hospital has a kidney transplant program that covers pre-transplant evaluation, donor workup, transplant surgery, and lifelong post-transplant follow-up. Our transplant team includes nephrologists, transplant surgeons, immunologists, and transplant coordinators. We perform both living-related and cadaver donor transplants. Patients are guided through the legal and medical clearance process, and post-transplant immunosuppression is managed by our nephrology team with regular monitoring of graft function.

help_outline What kidney stone treatments are available at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Hope Hospital offers the full spectrum of kidney stone treatments. Small stones (less than 5 mm) are managed conservatively with hydration and medication. For larger stones, we offer Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL), flexible ureteroscopy with laser lithotripsy, Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy (PCNL) for large or staghorn calculi, and Retrograde Intrarenal Surgery (RIRS). Our urologists use the latest holmium laser technology for precise stone fragmentation with minimal tissue damage. We also provide metabolic evaluation to identify causes of recurrent stones and create prevention plans.
