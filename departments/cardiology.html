About Our Cardiology Department in Nagpur

The Cardiology Department at Hope Hospital, Nagpur delivers comprehensive cardiovascular treatment through our strategic partnership with Zion Cardiac Hospital. This NABH-accredited collaboration brings together specialist doctors — experienced cardiologists, cardiac surgeons, and interventional specialists — under one roof, giving patients across Nagpur and Central India access to world-class heart care without traveling to metro cities.

Our cardiac care facility is equipped with a dedicated catheterization laboratory (cath lab) for diagnostic and interventional procedures, a heart-lung machine for open-heart surgeries, and advanced cardiac monitoring systems in our ICU. From preventive cardiology and risk assessment to complex bypass surgery and post-operative rehabilitation, our specialist medical team offers the complete spectrum of cardiac treatment and healthcare services.

Heart disease remains the leading cause of death in India, and early detection is critical for successful treatment. Our cardiology doctor team emphasizes preventive care through regular cardiac health checkups, risk factor screening, and patient education about heart-healthy lifestyles. When intervention is needed, our surgeons employ both traditional and minimally invasive approaches to deliver the best outcomes for every patient.

Our Cardiac Services

cardiology

Cardiac Surgery

Open-heart surgical procedures including coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), heart valve repair and replacement (mitral, aortic, tricuspid), congenital heart defect repair, and aortic aneurysm surgery. Performed using our advanced heart-lung machine for safe cardiopulmonary bypass.

monitor_heart

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive catheter-based procedures including coronary angioplasty with stent placement, balloon valvuloplasty, closure of septal defects, and peripheral vascular interventions. These procedures involve smaller incisions, shorter hospital stays, and faster recovery than open surgery.

radiology

Cath Lab Procedures

Our state-of-the-art catheterization laboratory performs diagnostic coronary angiography, fractional flow reserve (FFR) assessment, intravascular ultrasound (IVUS), and electrophysiology studies. The cath lab is available for both planned procedures and emergency interventions.

favorite

Heart Disease Treatment

Medical management of heart conditions including coronary artery disease, heart failure, atrial fibrillation, cardiomyopathy, and hypertensive heart disease. Our cardiologists create personalized treatment plans combining medication, lifestyle modification, and ongoing monitoring.

self_improvement

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Structured rehabilitation programs for patients recovering from heart attacks, cardiac surgery, or heart failure. Our program includes supervised exercise training, dietary counseling, stress management, and gradual return-to-activity guidance under cardiologist supervision.

electrical_services

Pacemaker Implant

Permanent pacemaker implantation for bradycardia and heart rhythm disorders, cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT) for heart failure patients, and implantable cardioverter-defibrillator (ICD) placement for patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest.

Cardiac Diagnostic Services

Accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective cardiac treatment. Our cardiology department offers a comprehensive range of non-invasive and invasive diagnostic investigations:

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG): Rapid assessment of heart rhythm and electrical activity
  • 2D Echocardiography: Detailed ultrasound imaging of heart structure and function
  • Treadmill Test (TMT): Stress testing to evaluate cardiac performance under exertion
  • Holter Monitoring: 24-48 hour continuous ECG recording for intermittent arrhythmias
  • Coronary Angiography: Gold-standard invasive imaging of coronary arteries via cath lab
  • Cardiac Biomarkers: Troponin, BNP, and other blood tests for heart damage and heart failure assessment

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline What cardiac procedures are available at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Hope Hospital's Cardiology Department, operating in partnership with Zion Cardiac Hospital, offers a full range of cardiac procedures including coronary angiography, coronary angioplasty with stenting, coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), heart valve repair and replacement, permanent pacemaker implantation, cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT), and implantable cardioverter-defibrillator (ICD) placement. Our state-of-the-art cath lab and heart-lung machine enable both diagnostic and interventional procedures with high precision.

help_outline Is heart surgery covered under Ayushman Bharat at Hope Hospital?

Yes, Hope Hospital is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) and provides free cardiac surgeries for eligible patients. This includes coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), heart valve replacement, pacemaker implantation, and other cardiac procedures. In addition to Ayushman Bharat, cardiac surgery is also covered under MJPJAY, CGHS, ECHS, and other government health schemes. Our empanelment team assists patients with documentation and eligibility verification for cashless treatment.

help_outline What are the warning signs of a heart attack?

Common warning signs of a heart attack include chest pain or discomfort (pressure, squeezing, fullness, or pain in the center or left side of the chest), shortness of breath, pain or discomfort in the jaw, neck, back, arms, or shoulder, feeling nauseous, light-headed, or unusually tired, and cold sweats. If you experience these symptoms, call our emergency number 1800-233-0000 immediately. Hope Hospital's emergency department operates 24/7 with cardiac care specialists available round the clock for timely intervention.
