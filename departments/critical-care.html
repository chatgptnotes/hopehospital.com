Critical Care & ICU – Specialist Doctors in Nagpur

Round-the-clock intensive care with 30 ICU beds, advanced life-support systems, ventilator management, and a dedicated team of intensivists ensuring the highest level of emergency and critical care in Nagpur.

About Our Critical Care Department in Nagpur

The Critical Care and ICU Department at Hope Hospital is the backbone of our NABH-accredited super specialty healthcare infrastructure. With 30 fully equipped ICU beds, our unit is one of the largest intensive care facilities in the Nagpur region, staffed by specialist doctors and intensivists trained to manage the most complex and life-threatening medical conditions. From multi-organ failure and severe trauma to post-surgical recovery and sepsis management, our critical care team delivers precise, evidence-based treatment around the clock.

Our ICU is staffed by a dedicated team of board-certified intensivists, critical care nurses, respiratory therapists, and support specialists who work in coordinated shifts to provide uninterrupted patient monitoring and intervention. Every bed station is equipped with multi-parameter monitors, mechanical ventilators, infusion pumps, and bedside diagnostic capability, enabling rapid decision-making and immediate response to any change in a patient's condition. Each specialist doctor is available at all hours, ensuring the highest standard of hospital healthcare.

Hope Hospital's critical care philosophy centers on early intervention, protocol-driven care, and family communication. We follow international guidelines for sepsis management, ventilator weaning, nutrition support, and infection control. Our ICU maintains strict protocols for hand hygiene, antimicrobial stewardship, and catheter-associated infection prevention, resulting in outcomes that meet national benchmarks for patient safety and recovery.

Our Critical Care Medical Services

bed

ICU Care (30 Beds)

Our 30-bed ICU provides individualized critical care for patients with multi-organ failure, severe infections, respiratory distress, neurological emergencies, and post-operative complications. Each bed station features advanced monitoring, isolation capability, and dedicated nursing care with a nurse-to-patient ratio optimized for critical cases.

air

Ventilator Support

Comprehensive mechanical ventilation services including invasive ventilation (endotracheal and tracheostomy), non-invasive ventilation (BiPAP/CPAP), and high-flow nasal cannula therapy. Our respiratory therapists manage ventilator weaning protocols to minimize ventilator-associated complications and support early recovery.

emergency

24/7 Emergency Response

Our emergency department operates round the clock with a rapid response team of intensivists, anesthesiologists, and emergency medicine specialists. Patients are triaged immediately upon arrival, with critical cases stabilized and transferred to the ICU within minutes. Pre-hospital ambulance coordination ensures seamless emergency care.

medical_services

Post-Operative Care

Specialized post-surgical intensive care for patients recovering from major cardiac, neurosurgical, orthopedic, and oncological procedures. Our team manages pain control, hemodynamic stability, wound care, and early mobilization to optimize surgical outcomes and reduce complications during the critical recovery period.

local_hospital

Trauma Care

Multi-disciplinary trauma management for road traffic accidents, falls, industrial injuries, and other traumatic emergencies. Our trauma team includes surgeons, intensivists, orthopedic specialists, and neurosurgeons who work together to provide immediate stabilization, damage control surgery, and definitive trauma care.

monitor_heart

Advanced Patient Monitoring

Continuous multi-parameter monitoring including ECG, SpO2, invasive and non-invasive blood pressure, central venous pressure (CVP), end-tidal CO2 capnography, and cardiac output measurement. Our centralized monitoring station enables real-time surveillance of all ICU patients with automated alerts for critical parameter deviations.

ICU Facilities & Equipment

Our Critical Care unit is equipped with the latest medical technology to deliver the highest standard of intensive care:

  • Multi-Parameter Patient Monitors: Continuous tracking of heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, respiratory rate, and temperature at every bed
  • Mechanical Ventilators: Advanced ICU ventilators with multiple modes (SIMV, PSV, PRVC, APRV) for both invasive and non-invasive ventilation
  • Central Oxygen & Medical Gas Supply: Pipeline oxygen, compressed air, and vacuum supply to all ICU beds with backup cylinder systems
  • Infusion Pumps & Syringe Drivers: Precise medication delivery systems for vasoactive drugs, sedation, insulin, and fluid management
  • Defibrillators & Crash Carts: Biphasic defibrillators and fully stocked emergency crash carts at designated stations throughout the ICU
  • Portable X-Ray & Ultrasound: Bedside imaging capability for rapid diagnostics without patient transport
  • Point-of-Care Testing (POCT): Bedside arterial blood gas analysis, blood glucose, lactate, and electrolyte measurement for immediate results
  • Central Monitoring Station: Networked surveillance system displaying real-time vitals of all 30 ICU patients with automated alarm escalation
  • Isolation Bays: Negative-pressure isolation rooms for infectious disease patients to prevent cross-contamination
  • Dialysis-Ready Beds: Designated ICU beds with plumbing and electrical infrastructure for bedside continuous renal replacement therapy (CRRT)

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline What ICU facilities are available at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Hope Hospital's Critical Care Department features 30 fully equipped ICU beds with multi-parameter patient monitors, mechanical ventilators (invasive and non-invasive), central oxygen and medical gas supply, infusion pumps, syringe drivers, defibrillators, and bedside point-of-care testing. Each ICU bed station is designed for comprehensive critical care with individual isolation capability, ensuring optimal infection control and patient safety.

help_outline What are the ICU visiting hours at Hope Hospital?

ICU visiting hours at Hope Hospital are structured to balance patient recovery with family communication. Visiting is permitted during designated hours: 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM and 5:00 PM to 5:30 PM daily. Only one attendant is allowed per patient at a time. The attending intensivist provides daily updates to the family during visiting hours. In critical situations, the medical team may allow additional visits at their discretion. Visitors must follow infection control protocols including hand hygiene and wearing provided protective gear.

help_outline How does the emergency admission process work at Hope Hospital?

Hope Hospital's emergency department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. On arrival, patients are immediately triaged by the emergency medical team to assess the severity of the condition. Critical patients are stabilized in the emergency room and transferred to the ICU within minutes. The hospital maintains a rapid response team of intensivists, anesthesiologists, and emergency medicine specialists available round the clock. For emergencies, call 1800-233-0000. Ambulance coordination and pre-arrival preparation are initiated upon receiving the emergency call.
