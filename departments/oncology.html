About Our Oncology Department in Nagpur

A cancer diagnosis demands a multi-specialist response, not a single consultation. Hope Hospital’s Oncology Department, in partnership with the Nagpur Cancer Institute, brings together medical oncologists, surgical oncologists, and palliative care specialists under one roof in Nagpur. Each case is reviewed through a multi-disciplinary tumour board, ensuring that treatment decisions — whether chemotherapy, surgery, supportive care, or a combination — are evidence-based and personalised to the individual patient.

Cancer treatment requires a multidisciplinary medical approach, and our Hope Hospital department is built on this principle. Every cancer case is reviewed by our tumor board -- a specialist team comprising surgical oncologists, medical oncologists, pathologists, and radiologists who collectively determine the most effective treatment strategy for each individual patient. This collaborative decision-making process ensures that patients receive the most appropriate and evidence-based healthcare care.

We understand that a cancer diagnosis affects not just the patient but the entire family. Our oncology team provides emotional support, nutritional counseling, pain management, and palliative care services alongside active treatment, ensuring that every patient's journey is handled with both medical expertise and genuine compassion.

Our Cancer Medical Services & Treatments

medication

Chemotherapy

Systemic chemotherapy for various cancer types administered in our dedicated chemotherapy suite under close medical supervision. We offer neo-adjuvant (pre-surgery), adjuvant (post-surgery), and palliative chemotherapy protocols with careful monitoring for side effects and treatment response.

radiology

Radiation Therapy (Referral)

Radiation therapy is arranged through referral to partner radiation oncology centres. Our team coordinates radiation planning alongside your surgery and chemotherapy — whether as primary treatment, adjuvant therapy after surgery, or palliative treatment for symptom relief in advanced cancers.

medical_services

Surgical Oncology

Cancer surgery performed by experienced surgical oncologists in our modular operation theaters. We offer curative resections, debulking procedures, and minimally invasive laparoscopic oncological surgeries for breast, gastrointestinal, head and neck, urological, and gynecological cancers.

Free Cancer Treatment in Nagpur — Ayushman Bharat & MJPJAY

Hope Hospital is committed to making cancer treatment accessible to all. We are empaneled under multiple government health schemes that cover cancer treatment, enabling eligible patients to receive care at no cost:

  • Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY): Free cancer surgery and treatment for families below the poverty line with coverage up to Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year
  • MJPJAY (Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana): Maharashtra state scheme covering cancer surgeries and chemotherapy for eligible patients
  • CGHS: Central Government Health Scheme covering cancer treatment for government employees and pensioners
  • ECHS: Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme covering comprehensive cancer care for armed forces veterans

Our empanelment team assists every patient with eligibility verification, documentation, and cashless treatment processing. Contact us at 0712-2980073 / +91-9823555053 to check your eligibility.

help_outline What types of cancer does Hope Hospital treat?

Through our partnership with Nagpur Cancer Institute, Hope Hospital treats a wide range of cancers including breast cancer, lung cancer, colorectal cancer, head and neck cancers, gastrointestinal cancers (stomach, liver, pancreatic), urological cancers (kidney, bladder, prostate), gynecological cancers (cervical, ovarian, uterine), blood cancers (leukemia, lymphoma), bone and soft tissue tumors, and brain tumors. help_outline What cancer screening tests are available at Hope Hospital Nagpur?

Hope Hospital offers comprehensive cancer screening packages for early detection of various cancers. Available screenings include mammography and breast examination for breast cancer, Pap smear and HPV testing for cervical cancer, PSA blood test for prostate cancer, colonoscopy for colorectal cancer, low-dose CT scan for lung cancer in high-risk individuals, and tumor marker blood tests (CEA, CA-125, AFP, CA 19-9). We also offer comprehensive cancer risk assessment consultations where our oncologists evaluate family history, lifestyle factors, and genetic predisposition. Early detection significantly improves treatment outcomes, so we encourage regular screening for all adults above 40 years.
