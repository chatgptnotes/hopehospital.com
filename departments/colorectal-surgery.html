Specialized Colorectal & Proctology Care in Nagpur

The Colorectal Surgery department at NABH-accredited Hope Hospital Nagpur is dedicated exclusively to conditions of the colon, rectum, and anus. Our specialist surgeon, Dr. Akshay Akulwar, leads a focused team that delivers deeper expertise, better treatment outcomes, and genuine understanding of the sensitive nature of these conditions.

From common problems like piles and fissures -- which affect millions but are often neglected due to embarrassment -- to complex colorectal cancers requiring multidisciplinary medical management, our specialist doctor Dr. Akulwar and his team provide confidential, compassionate, and technically excellent care for every patient.

Our Medical Services & Treatments

Colorectal Cancer Management

Complete cancer care from screening and diagnosis through surgical resection, chemotherapy coordination, and long-term surveillance. Laparoscopic resections (right/left hemicolectomy, anterior resection, APR) reduce recovery time. All cases reviewed by our multidisciplinary tumor board.

healing

IBD Treatment

Medical and surgical management of Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis. We coordinate with gastroenterologists for optimized biologic therapy, and offer surgical options including strictureplasty, bowel resection, and ileal pouch-anal anastomosis (IPAA) when medical management is insufficient.

accessibility_new

Pelvic Floor Disorders

Evaluation and treatment of rectal prolapse, rectocele, obstructed defecation syndrome, and fecal incontinence. We use anorectal manometry and defecography for precise diagnosis, followed by targeted surgical repair including ventral mesh rectopexy and sphincter repair.

medical_services

Proctology -- Piles, Fissures & Fistulas

Full range of treatment from conservative management to advanced procedures: rubber band ligation, stapled hemorrhoidopexy (MIPH), HAL-RAR for hemorrhoids; lateral internal sphincterotomy for fissures; fistulotomy, LIFT procedure, and advancement flaps for complex anal fistulas.

precision_manufacturing

Laparoscopic Colorectal Surgery

Minimally invasive approach to colorectal resections that reduces pain, hospital stay, and recovery time compared with open surgery. Dr. Akulwar's UK training includes extensive laparoscopic experience, ensuring precise oncological resection margins through small incisions.

Conditions We Treat

Our colorectal surgery department manages a wide spectrum of conditions:

  • Hemorrhoids (piles) -- all grades
  • Anal fissures (acute and chronic)
  • Anal fistulas (simple, complex, and recurrent)
  • Pilonidal sinus disease
  • Rectal prolapse and rectocele
  • Colorectal cancer (colon cancer and rectal cancer)
  • Colorectal polyps and familial adenomatous polyposis (FAP)
  • Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis
  • Diverticular disease and diverticulitis
  • Fecal incontinence and obstructed defecation
  • Perianal abscess

Why Choose Hope Hospital for Colorectal Surgery?

Colorectal conditions carry a stigma that often delays treatment. At Hope Hospital, we have built a department where patients feel comfortable seeking medical help. Consultations are conducted privately by our specialist doctor team, and our healthcare professionals explain every treatment procedure in plain language so patients can make informed decisions.

For colorectal cancer, our strength is multidisciplinary care. Every cancer case is presented at our tumor board, where surgeons, medical oncologists, radiation oncologists, radiologists, and pathologists collectively determine the optimal treatment sequence. This collaborative approach has been shown to improve survival outcomes and reduce treatment complications.

Dr. Akulwar's UK training also means our surgical techniques stay current with international best practices. From total mesorectal excision (TME) with nerve preservation to the latest minimally invasive approaches, patients at Hope Hospital receive care that follows global evidence-based guidelines.

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline Who is Dr. Akshay Akulwar and what is his expertise?

Dr. Akshay Akulwar is a UK-trained colorectal surgeon practicing at Hope Hospital Nagpur. He completed his advanced training in colorectal surgery in the United Kingdom, where he gained extensive experience in laparoscopic colorectal resections, complex proctology, pelvic floor reconstruction, and multidisciplinary colorectal cancer management. Dr. Akulwar brings international surgical standards to Nagpur, offering evidence-based treatment for conditions ranging from common proctological problems like piles, fissures, and fistulas to advanced colorectal cancers and inflammatory bowel disease.

help_outline What treatment options are available for piles (hemorrhoids) at Hope Hospital?

Hope Hospital offers a comprehensive range of hemorrhoid treatments tailored to the grade and severity of the condition. Grade I and II hemorrhoids are managed with dietary modification, medications, and office-based procedures such as rubber band ligation and sclerotherapy. Grade III and IV hemorrhoids may require surgical intervention including stapled hemorrhoidopexy (MIPH), Hemorrhoidal Artery Ligation (HAL-RAR), and conventional hemorrhoidectomy. Dr. Akshay Akulwar evaluates each case individually and recommends the least invasive effective treatment, prioritizing faster recovery and lower recurrence rates.

help_outline Does Hope Hospital treat colorectal cancer with laparoscopic surgery?

Yes. Dr. Akshay Akulwar performs laparoscopic colorectal cancer resections at Hope Hospital, including right and left hemicolectomy, sigmoid colectomy, anterior resection, and abdominoperineal resection. Laparoscopic surgery for colorectal cancer offers smaller incisions, less blood loss, reduced post-operative pain, shorter hospital stays, and faster return to chemotherapy when needed. All colorectal cancer cases are discussed in our multidisciplinary tumor board with oncologists, radiation oncologists, radiologists, and pathologists to ensure the best treatment plan for each patient.
