What is Ayushman Bharat?

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) is India's biggest government health scheme. The central government gives your family up to Rs. 5 lakhs every year for hospital treatment completely free of cost. You don't need to arrange money before admission. The hospital gets paid directly by the government.

In Maharashtra, there is also MJPJAY (Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana) a state government scheme that works alongside PM-JAY. Many families in Nagpur who don't qualify under PM-JAY can still get free treatment under MJPJAY. Hope Hospital is empaneled under both.

Am I Eligible for Ayushman Bharat? How to Check

Not every family automatically gets this card. Here is how to find out if you qualify it only takes a few minutes:

  1. Call 14555 the free PM-JAY helpline (available in Hindi, Marathi, and English). Give them your mobile or ration card number and they will tell you immediately.
  2. Visit Hope Hospital our insurance desk will check your eligibility on the spot. Just bring your Aadhar card. No appointment needed.
  3. Check online on the PM-JAY official website using your mobile number or ration card number.
  4. Visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC/Jan Seva Kendra) they will check and help you apply.

Who is usually eligible? Families listed in the 2011 SECC survey, daily wage workers, farmers, construction workers, sanitation workers, and other recognized occupational groups. If your family is below poverty line or has a BPL ration card, you are very likely eligible.

Common Questions People Ask (FAQ)

Is Hope Hospital on the Ayushman Bharat hospital list in Nagpur?

Yes. Hope Hospital at Teka Naka, Kamptee Road, Nagpur is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, and 20+ government health schemes. If you have a valid Ayushman card, your surgery and treatment here is completely free.

How do I know if I have Ayushman Bharat benefits?

The easiest way is to call 14555 (free helpline) and give your mobile or ration card number. Or just come to Hope Hospital with your Aadhar card our insurance team will check in minutes.

Which surgeries are free at Hope Hospital under Ayushman Bharat?

Knee replacement, hip replacement, heart surgery (angioplasty, bypass), cancer surgery, brain tumor surgery, kidney dialysis, kidney stone surgery, laparoscopic surgery, piles surgery, and many more all free for eligible patients.

What documents should I bring to the hospital?

Bring your Ayushman card (or e-card on phone), Aadhar card, ration card, and any old medical reports you have. If you don't have a card yet, bring Aadhar and ration card we will generate the card for you.

What is the difference between PM-JAY and MJPJAY?

PM-JAY is from the central government it covers families below poverty line across India. MJPJAY is from the Maharashtra state government it covers more families in Maharashtra who may not qualify under PM-JAY. Hope Hospital is empaneled under both, so more patients can get free treatment.

Can I use Ayushman Bharat for an emergency at night?

Yes. Hope Hospital is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. If you come with an emergency, treatment begins immediately. The Ayushman paperwork is handled by our team while you are being treated you don't have to worry about it.

How long does it take to get approval?

For planned surgery, approval usually comes in 1 to 2 hours. For emergencies, we start treatment right away and get approval at the same time. You never have to wait for treatment because of paperwork.
