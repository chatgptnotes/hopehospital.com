What is Ayushman Bharat?

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) is the world's largest government-funded health insurance scheme, launched by the Government of India. It provides health coverage of up to Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalisation.

In Maharashtra, PM-JAY works alongside Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY) — the state government's health scheme — which extends coverage to even more families across Maharashtra including Nagpur.

How to Check if You Are Eligible for Ayushman Bharat

Not every family is automatically covered. Here is how to check your eligibility:

  1. Visit the official PM-JAY website and enter your mobile number or ration card number
  2. Call the PM-JAY helpline: 14555 — available in Hindi, Marathi, and English
  3. Visit Hope Hospital directly — our insurance desk will check your eligibility on the spot using your Aadhar card
  4. Check at your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) with your Aadhar card

If your family was listed in the 2011 Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) or you belong to a recognized occupational category, you are likely eligible.

Documents Required for Ayushman Bharat Treatment
