Hope Hospital Nagpur: Central India's NABH-Accredited Super Specialty Hospital Expands Services in 2026

Hope Hospital Nagpur — established 2004, NABH-accredited since 2012 — announces expanded multispecialty care covering maternity & NICU, neurosurgery, cardiac care, cancer treatment, and kidney transplant, all available free under Ayushman Bharat.

For Immediate Release  |  May 13, 2026  |  Media enquiries: cmd@hopehospital.com  |  0712-2980073

Nagpur, Maharashtra — May 13, 2026

Hope Hospital Nagpur, Central India's only NABH-accredited super specialty hospital, today announced a significant expansion of its multispecialty care offerings for 2026. The hospital, which has served over 5 lakh patients since its founding in 2004, now provides advanced treatment across maternity and NICU, neurosurgery, cardiac care, cancer treatment, and kidney transplant — all available at no cost for eligible patients under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) and 20+ government health schemes.

Located at Teka Naka, Kamptee Road, Nagpur, Hope Hospital combines NABH quality standards with the widest government scheme empanelment among private super specialty hospitals in the Vidarbha region, making world-class care accessible to every section of society.

All Specialties Available FREE Under Ayushman Bharat

Eligible patients under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, ECHS, and 17+ other government schemes receive comprehensive multispecialty treatment at zero cost at Hope Hospital. Call 0712-2980073 / +91-9823555053 to verify eligibility.

About Hope Hospital Nagpur

Hope Hospital, Nagpur is Central India's first and only NABH-accredited comprehensive super specialty hospital. Founded in 2004 and NABH-accredited since 2012, the hospital operates a 200-bed facility with 30 ICU beds, 3 modular operation theatres, and 24,000 sq ft of clinical space at Teka Naka, Kamptee Road, Nagpur – 440026. Hope Hospital offers advanced care across orthopedics, cardiology (Zion Cardiac), oncology (Nagpur Cancer Institute), nephrology (NephroPlus dialysis and kidney transplant), neurosurgery (Hope Neurology), maternity and NICU, gastroenterology, and critical care. The hospital is empaneled under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) and 20+ government health schemes, enabling over 5 lakh patients to access specialist treatment free of cost. Led by Dr. B.K. Murali (MS Ortho, Lokmat Healthcare Award winner, 5,000+ surgeries) and a team of visiting super specialists, Hope Hospital has earned 402+ verified Google reviews. For more information, visit www.hopehospital.com or call 0712-2980073.
