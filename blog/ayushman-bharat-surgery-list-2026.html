What is Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY)?

Ayushman Bharat — Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) is the world's largest government-funded health insurance scheme. Launched in 2018 by the Government of India, it provides cashless hospitalization up to Rs. 5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary medical care at empaneled hospitals across India.

Under PMJAY, 12.34 crore eligible families (about 55 crore people) receive free treatment for 1,949 medical procedures across 27 specialties — including all major surgeries like heart bypass, kidney transplant, cancer chemotherapy, knee replacement, brain surgery, and more.

Key Facts About Ayushman Bharat 2026

  • Cover amount: Rs. 5 lakh per family per year (no per-individual sub-limit)
  • Cashless: Zero out-of-pocket cost at empaneled hospitals
  • Procedures covered: 1,949 (all major surgeries + chemotherapy + dialysis + maternity)
  • Hospitals empaneled: 30,000+ across India (1,800+ in Maharashtra)
  • Recent expansion: All citizens above age 70 now automatically eligible (since Oct 2024) regardless of income
  • Pre-existing diseases: Covered from day 1
  • 3 days pre + 15 days post-hospitalization expenses covered

Who is Eligible for Ayushman Bharat in 2026?

Three main eligibility categories:

1. Families identified by SECC 2011

The Socio-Economic Caste Census 2011 identified families in deprivation categories. Most rural BPL families and urban occupation-based workers (street vendors, construction labour, domestic help, etc.) are automatically eligible.

2. Senior Citizens 70 years and above (New from Oct 2024)

All Indian citizens aged 70+ are now automatically covered under PMJAY, regardless of income or social category — known as the "Ayushman Vandana Yojana" expansion. They get a separate Rs. 5 lakh top-up.

3. State-Specific Scheme Beneficiaries

Several states converged their existing schemes with PMJAY. Maharashtra residents also benefit from MJPJAY (Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana), which extends coverage to additional families (yellow & orange ration card holders) and provides Rs. 1.5 lakh additional cover for renal diseases.

Documents Needed for Free Surgery

Bring these documents on the day of admission or OPD visit:

  1. Aadhaar card of the patient (mandatory for biometric verification)
  2. Ayushman Bharat card / e-card / PMJAY ID number (digital copy on phone is acceptable)
  3. Ration card or family ID (for family relationship verification)
  4. Doctor's prescription / referral recommending the surgery
  5. Previous medical records: reports, X-rays, scans, blood tests, discharge summaries
  6. Mobile number registered for PMJAY (for OTP verification)

Important — Pre-Authorization Required

For planned surgery, the hospital must submit a pre-authorization request to the State Health Authority (SHA) along with diagnosis, package code, estimated cost, and supporting reports. Approval typically takes 6-48 hours. For emergencies, treatment starts immediately and pre-auth is processed in parallel.

MJPJAY — Maharashtra's Package List (2026)

MJPJAY (Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana) is Maharashtra's own health-assurance scheme, now converged with Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY) and administered by the State Health Assurance Society. For Maharashtra residents, the two schemes work together — most government beneficiaries are covered under the combined AB-PMJAY + MJPJAY card.

  • Who's covered: SECC families under PMJAY, plus yellow & orange ration card holders and other categories under MJPJAY.
  • Cover: Up to Rs. 5 lakh per family per year under PMJAY, with MJPJAY adding cover for further procedures and renal (dialysis/transplant) care.
  • Package list & rates: The MJPJAY procedure and package list is published on the Maharashtra State Health Assurance Society portal — search "MJPJAY package list" on the official state site, or ask our PMJAY desk to look up your procedure.

Hope Hospital, Nagpur is empaneled under both PMJAY and MJPJAY, so eligible Maharashtra patients receive cashless treatment under whichever scheme applies to them.

Hope Hospital, Nagpur is empaneled under both PMJAY and MJPJAY — call 0712-2980073 and our desk will confirm the package for your procedure.

Q: Where can I find the Ayushman Bharat / PMJAY package code list (procedure codes)?

Every procedure under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) has a unique package code in the National Health Authority's Health Benefit Package (HBP) — the official code list of all 1,949 procedures with their package codes and amounts, published as a downloadable PDF/Excel at nha.gov.in and pmjay.gov.in (Health Benefit Packages). Maharashtra's MJPJAY procedure codes are on the State Health Assurance Society portal. Don't know your procedure's code? Hope Hospital's PMJAY desk will look up the exact package code and amount for your surgery and print it for you, free — call 0712-2980073.
