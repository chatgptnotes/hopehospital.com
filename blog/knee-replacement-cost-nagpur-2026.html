Factors That Affect Knee Replacement Cost in Nagpur

The implant is the single biggest cost driver in knee replacement surgery. Here's a comparison:

Dr. B.K. Murali's recommendation: The right implant depends on your age, weight, activity level, and bone quality — not just cost. During consultation, he discusses all options transparently so you can make an informed decision.

2. Single vs Bilateral (Both Knees)

Many patients need both knees replaced. At Hope Hospital, bilateral knee replacement (both knees in one surgery) is available, which typically costs 60–70% of doing them separately — saving money and reducing total recovery time.

3. Hospital Tier & Infrastructure

Hope Hospital is NABH-accredited with 3 modular OTs, a dedicated ICU, and advanced imaging — which gives better outcomes at competitive prices compared to smaller clinics that may quote lower but lack infrastructure for complications.

4. Patient's Medical Condition

Patients with diabetes, heart disease, obesity, or previous knee surgeries may need additional pre-surgical workup, longer ICU stay, or specialized anesthesia — which can add ₹20,000–₹50,000 to the total cost.
