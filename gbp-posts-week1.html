Kidney Warning Signs

Wednesday, 13 May 2026 · Health Tip
✅ PUBLISHED
Post Text
Your kidneys filter 200 litres of blood every day — but they rarely show symptoms until serious damage is done. 🚨 Warning signs you should never ignore: ▪ Swelling in feet, ankles or face ▪ Foamy or dark-coloured urine ▪ Frequent urination at night ▪ Persistent fatigue & weakness ▪ High blood pressure that won't control ▪ Loss of appetite & nausea If you have any of these symptoms, get a kidney function test done immediately. Early detection saves kidneys. Endoscopy sounds scary. It isn't. An endoscopy is a simple, safe procedure where a thin flexible camera is passed through the mouth to examine your food pipe, stomach & intestine. When do you need one? ▪ Persistent acidity or heartburn ▪ Difficulty swallowing ▪ Unexplained weight loss ▪ Blood in vomit or stool ▪ Stomach ulcer follow-up ✅ No surgery. No stitches. Stroke Emergency Awareness

Sunday, 17 May 2026 · Emergency Awareness
✅ PUBLISHED
Post Text
Every minute counts during a stroke. Know the signs — act immediately. Use the FAST method: 🔴 F — Face drooping on one side 🔴 A — Arm weakness or numbness 🔴 S — Speech slurred or confused 🔴 T — Time to call emergency NOW Other warning signs: ▪ Sudden severe headache with no cause ▪ Sudden vision loss in one or both eyes ▪ Loss of balance or coordination The first 4.5 hours are the golden window for stroke treatment. After that, brain damage becomes irreversible. Hope Hospital's 24/7 neurosurgery & emergency team is always ready at Kamptee Road, Nagpur.
