About Hope Hospital

Central India's Premier Super Specialty Healthcare Center

Delivering world-class medical care with compassion and excellence for over 19 years

NABH Accredited Excellence Since 2012

Hope Hospital stands as Central India's first NABH-accredited comprehensive super specialty center, providing exceptional healthcare services to over 5 million people within a 500 km radius.

Led by the renowned Dr. B.K. Murali, an esteemed orthopedic and spine surgeon, our facility combines cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate patient care across our 100-bed, 24,000 sq ft state-of-the-art facility.
