Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hope Hospital the best hospital in Nagpur? +

Yes, Hope Hospital is recognized as the best hospital in Nagpur and Central India's first NABH-accredited comprehensive super specialty center. We have been providing exceptional healthcare services for over 19 years with a team of 20+ specialist consultants, 200+ beds, and state-of-the-art medical equipment including cath lab, dialysis unit, and modular operation theaters.

Is Dr. B.K. Murali the best orthopedic surgeon in Nagpur? +

Yes, Dr. B.K. Murali is widely recognized as the best orthopedic surgeon in Nagpur and Central India. He is an M.S. (Ortho) Joint Replacement Surgeon with over 19 years of experience, specializing in total knee replacement, total hip replacement, spine surgery, arthroscopy, and sports medicine. He has won the Lokmat Visionary & Innovator Healthcare Award and is a finalist in India's Innovation Growth Programme.

What are the visiting hours and OPD timings at Hope Hospital? +

Hope Hospital operates 24/7 for emergency services. Our Morning OPD is available from 8 AM to 11 AM every day, featuring specialized clinics for Cardiology, Cancer, Orthopedics, Brain Tumor, and Gastroenterology. For appointments, call 071-22980073 / +91-9823555053 or book online at betserlife.com.

Is Hope Hospital empaneled under Ayushman Bharat and other government schemes? +

Yes, Hope Hospital is empaneled under 20+ government health schemes including Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, ECHS, Maharashtra Police Kutumb Arogya Yojana, Central Railways, Coal India Limited, and many others. We provide free surgeries for cancer, heart, orthopedic, brain tumor, abdominal, and bladder procedures under these schemes. Cashless treatment is available for all empaneled patients.

What insurance companies and TPAs are accepted at Hope Hospital? +

Hope Hospital accepts 9+ major insurance companies including ICICI Lombard, Reliance Health Insurance, United Healthcare India, MaxBupa, TATA AIG, Star Health, HDFC ERGO, and Royal Sundaram. We are also empaneled with 10+ TPAs including MD India Health Insurance, Family Health Plan, Raksha Health Insurance, and others. Cashless treatment is available for all network patients.

What advanced medical equipment and facilities are available at Hope Hospital? +

Hope Hospital is equipped with Heart Lung Machine, Laparoscope & Arthroscope, Clean Dialysis Unit, C-arm Flatpanel, Cath Lab, Carl Zeiss Microscope, Endoscopy, Colonoscopy, Anesthesia Workstation, Modular Operation Theaters, Ultrasound, 24x7 Pathology Lab, 24x7 Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, X-ray & Imaging, and 24x7 Emergency services. We have 200+ beds including ICU facilities and 3 modular OTs spanning 24,000 sq ft.

How do I book an appointment with Dr. B.K. Murali or other specialists? +

You can book appointments through three ways: 1) Online booking at betserlife.com, 2) Call our hotline at 071-22980073 / +91-9823555053, or 3) Visit our Morning OPD from 8 AM to 11 AM at Hope Hospital Teka Naka, Ayushman Nagpur Hospital Ramdaspeth, or Ayushman Polyclinic Pandurna.

What are the locations of Hope Hospital in Nagpur? +

Hope Hospital operates three healthcare facilities: 1) Hope Hospital – Teka Naka: Near Go-Gas Auto LPG-2, Teka Naka, Kamptee Road, Nagpur – 440026 (main 100-bed super specialty hospital), 2) Ayushman Nagpur Hospital: 3rd Floor, Shrivardhan Complex, Lokmat Square, Ramdaspeth, Nagpur – 440012, and 3) Ayushman Polyclinic Pandurna: Pandurna, Chhindwara District, Madhya Pradesh. All locations are open 24/7 for emergency services.

Which is the best maternity hospital in Nagpur for normal delivery and C-section? +

Hope Hospital is among the best maternity hospitals in Nagpur, offering normal delivery, C-section, painless delivery with epidural analgesia, high-risk pregnancy management, and an on-site NICU. Normal delivery costs ₹20,000–₹40,000 for private patients; deliveries are free under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, and ECHS. The gynecology team also treats PCOD, fibroids, endometriosis, and ovarian cysts. Call 071-22980073 for a maternity package.

Which is the best hospital for brain tumour, stroke, and neurosurgery in Nagpur? +

Hope Hospital houses Hope Neurology, a dedicated neurosurgery center offering 24/7 brain and spine care. Services include brain tumour surgery, emergency stroke treatment within the golden hour, spine surgery, epilepsy surgery, head injury management, and hydrocephalus treatment. A Neuro ICU is available on-site. Neurosurgery is covered free under Ayushman Bharat and MJPJAY. Recognize stroke with FAST: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call 071-22980073.

What is the cost of knee replacement surgery in Nagpur? +

Knee replacement surgery in Nagpur costs ₹1.5 lakh to ₹3.5 lakh for private patients depending on implant type. At Hope Hospital, patients eligible under Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY), MJPJAY, CGHS, ECHS, and 17+ other schemes receive total knee replacement completely free. Dr. B.K. Murali — MS Ortho, 19+ years, 5000+ surgeries — leads the orthopedic team. Call 071-22980073 to check your eligibility.

Is Hope Hospital NABH accredited and what does NABH mean for patients? +

Yes, Hope Hospital is NABH accredited since 2012 — one of the first NABH-accredited comprehensive super specialty hospitals in Central India. NABH is the highest quality standard in Indian healthcare, covering patient safety, infection control, clinical care protocols, and equipment maintenance. NABH-accredited hospitals are preferred by Ayushman Bharat, CGHS, and all major insurance companies for cashless treatment.
